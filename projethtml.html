Work

I am Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time,i have won five Ballon d'Or awards,a record three UEFA Best Player in Europe, and four European Golden Shoes,the most by a European player.I have won 33 trophies in my career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions Leagues, the UEFA European Championship and the UEFA Nations League.
